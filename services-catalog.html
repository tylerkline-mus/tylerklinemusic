Loose Leaf Transmissions
Audio Production Services
LLT is an audio-forward production platform rooted in the ethos of public media. When organizations hire LLT, they’re commissioning original audio work built specifically for them — in the same spirit as Living Classical and music/Maker, serving the same goal: helping their audiences feel like they belong to the music.

Living Classical and music/Maker are fully editorially independent and are not available for purchase or sponsorship. They appear here as demonstration of the philosophy — proof of concept for what LLT is capable of creating.
